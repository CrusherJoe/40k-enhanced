# Boogeyman archetype — Dark Angels Deathwing Terminator brick

GT-era list ("playing Thousand Sons with Dark Angels models" = it grinds up the
board like an un-killable deathstar). Stats from BSData (`Imperium - Dark
Angels.cat` rev51 + base Space Marines). This is the **elite / low-model /
invuln-brick** pole — few models, extreme quality, near-immovable.

## The list (2000) — Unforgiven Task Force + Librarius Conclave (Take and Hold)
- **3× Deathwing Knights** (240–260 ea): **T5 W4 Sv2+ / 4++**, 5-model, Mace of
  Absolution S6 AP-2 D2 A4 (Anti-Vehicle/Monster 4+) + mortal-wound output; *Watcher in
  the Dark* tanks a mortal once/game. Each led by a **Librarian in Terminator** (T5 W5
  2+) + **Ancient in Terminator** (thunder hammer + storm shield, 2+/4++). ≈ three
  ~30-wound 2+/4++ melee anchors that Deep Strike.
- **Sternguard (10) + Librarian** (Fusillade): Bolt rifle **S4 AP-1 Dev Wounds**, 2 heavy
  bolters — anti-infantry that ignores my armour on 6s-to-wound.
- **Repulsor**: **T12 W16 3+**, Twin lascannon + Las-talon + Hunter-slayer, all **AP-3
  anti-tank** — snipes my transports/Paragons.
- Eradicators (Gravis, multi-melta variant), Land Speeder (multi-melta), Lieutenant,
  **Scout Squad** (infiltrate, sniper, actions).

## Threat profile → what it forces MY list to do
**1. Durability — high-quality + invuln, but W4 (not W16).** 3× 2+/4++ Terminator bricks
+ Repulsor. Like C'tan/Monoliths, **the 4++ blunts my AP** (AP-4 = still a 4++). *But*
Deathwing are **W4**, so **melta's multi-damage removes a whole model per failed 4++** —
**melta stays efficient here** (unlike vs W16 C'tan). And the Repulsor (no invuln) just
dies to AP-4. → Bring melta, accept the 4++, lean on Miracle dice for the key wound/save,
and expect a grind against 3 bricks.

**2. What kills my girls — Deep-Striking melee bricks + AP-3 anti-tank.** Maces + thunder
hammers + Lethal-Hits relic weapons (with mortals) that **teleport in**; if a brick
reaches my line, my T3 W1 girls are ground to paste. Plus lascannon/las-talon snipe my
transports and Sternguard Dev-Wounds shred infantry. → **Don't get charged by the bricks;
screen the Deep Strike; treat transports as expendable delivery, not cover.**

**3. Objective game — DURABLE ≠ SCORING.** The killer insight: **Deathwing Knights are
OC1** — a 5-brick is only OC5, and there are just 3 of them. Their durability does NOT buy
objective control. → **I out-OC them.** Flood objectives with cheap OC2 Battle Sisters /
Sacresants / Paragons; the bricks can't be everywhere. Win Take-and-Hold on bodies while
refusing to feed the melee. Kill the *killable* (Sternguard, Eradicators, Speeder,
Scouts, isolated characters).

## The lesson — "durable ≠ scoring", and melta still works on W4
1. **Out-OC elite armies.** Every elite brick faction (Deathwing, and partly Custodes)
   trades bodies for quality — so their *board control is thin*. Cheap Sisters OC bodies
   contest what the bricks can't reach. This is a general Sisters edge vs the whole
   top-heavy field.
2. **Melta scales with wounds-per-model.** vs W4 terminators melta's D6 removes a model
   per unsaved 4++ (efficient); vs W16 C'tan it's a rounding error (don't bother). Same
   AP-4, opposite verdict — **judge melta by the target's wounds, not just its save.**
3. **My transports keep showing up on the menu** (Ork rokkits → Necron death rays → DA
   lascannons). They are delivery + ablative wounds, **not invincible** — extract value
   before they pop.
