# Boogeyman archetypes — Orks (two poles)

Two GT-era Ork lists that bracket the faction. Stats ingested live from BSData
(`Xenos - Orks.cat` rev162). Together they define the **melta↔flamer axis** a
take-all-comers Sisters list must straddle.

## List 1 — Kult of Speed + More Dakka! (Disruption / Purge the Foe): mobile dakka
| Unit | Stats | Threat weapon |
|---|---|---|
| Deffkoptas ×3 (6 ea = 18) | **T6 W4 4+ M12"** | Kopta rokkits S9 **AP-2** D3 Blast, Twin-linked (anti-elite/tank) |
| Flash Gitz ×2 (10 ea) | T5 W2 4+ | Snazzgun S6 **AP-1** D2 A3 Heavy, Sustained 1 |
| Tankbustas | T5 W2 4+ | Rokkit launcha S9 **AP-2** D3 Blast — **anti-vehicle, pops my transports** |
| Kill Rig ×2 | **T10 W16 3+** | Wurrtower S12 AP-3 D6 **Torrent**; Stikka kannon S12 AP-2 **Anti-Vehicle 2+** |
| Wazdakka | T8 W10 3+ M14" | Psyko-gatler S6 AP-2 D2 **A12** |
| Big Mek Shokk Gun | T5 W5 4+ | Shokk attack gun S9 **AP-4** D6 Blast (random anti-tank) |
| 3× Trukk, 3× Gretchin | T8 W10 4+ / chaff | mobility + action/OC bodies |

**Threat profile.** *Durability:* a few genuinely tough hulls (Kill Rigs T10/W16,
Wazdakka, Trukks) but the bulk is **T6 W4 koptas / T5 W2 shooty mobs** — wants **AP-2
volume + multi-damage** more than pure AP-4. *What kills my girls:* a lot of fast,
mid-strength **AP-1/-2 dakka at range** that also carries **anti-tank rokkits — so my
Immolators are NOT safe ablative walls here.** *Objective game:* Disruption — koptas +
trukks + grots out-maneuver and do actions. **I need speed to contest and must trade
transports carefully.**

## List 2 — Green Tide + Taktikal Brigade (Take and Hold): the body mountain
| Unit | Stats | Note |
|---|---|---|
| **5× Boyz (20 each = 100)** | **T5 W1 Sv5+ OC2** | Choppa S4 **AP-1** A3; **Power klaw** Nob S9 AP-2 D2. Painboy → **Feel No Pain** |
| Ghazghkull + Makari | **T6 W10 2+** | Gork's Klaw S14 AP-3 D4; tanky melee monster |
| Warboss ×2 / Bigboss ×3 | T5 W6 4+ | Power klaw S10 AP-2 D2; buff auras |
| Painboy ×3 | T5 W3 5+ | grants FNP to the mob |
| Kommandos (10) | T5 W1 5+ | infiltrate; power klaw + breacha ram + burna |
| Stormboyz, Gretchin, Wazdakka | jump / chaff | screens + fast + OC |

**Threat profile.** *Durability:* per-model trivial (T5 W1 Sv5+) but **~100 wounds +
Feel No Pain + OC2 apiece** and a couple of tough characters (Ghaz T6 W10 2+). **Melta is
massively over-invested — you cannot AP-4 through 100 bodies.** This is where **flamers
(Torrent auto-hit, ignore cover), Blast, and AP-1 volume** (heavy bolters, Castigator
battle cannon, Exorcist) clear the tide; a heavy bolter's AP-1 D2 murders W1 Boyz.
*What kills my girls:* a **melee tsunami** — 100 choppas + power klaws + Warbosses
charging; my T3 W1 girls evaporate in combat and get swamped on OC. **I must thin them at
range before they arrive, screen, use overwatch/Fights-First, and never get tied up.**
*Objective game:* Take and Hold — I **cannot out-body them**; I must kill bodies to break
the hold and win on secondaries + positioning.

## THE SYNTHESIS — the melta↔flamer axis (why this matters most)
The three archetypes so far define a spectrum my ONE list must cover:

```
   MELTA (AP-4) needed  <───────────────────────────>  FLAMER/BLAST/volume needed
   Salamanders LRs,        Ork Kill Rigs/Wazdakka,        Ork Green Tide
   2+/multiwound anchors    T6 mid bodies                 100× T5 W1
```

- **Mono-flamer (Bringers of Flame / Purge)** → loses the left half: bounces off 2+
  Land Raiders & AP-2 does nothing to reduce their tough hulls, and it walks fragile
  bodies into Ork melee range. **Disfavored for all-comers.**
- **Mono-melta spam** → wasted vs Green Tide; can't kill 100 bodies with D6-damage guns.
- **Answer = a balanced toolkit across SPECIALIZED units** (never mixed within a unit,
  per the loadout rule):
  - *All-melta* — Dominion melta-half in Immolator, multi-melta Retributors, Paragon
    multi-meltas, inferno-pistol characters → anchors/vehicles/2+.
  - *All-flamer / Blast* — Dominion flamer-half, heavy-flamer Retributors, Immolator
    Immolation flamers, **Castigator battle cannon (Blast AP-1)**, Zephyrim/Seraphim →
    hordes.
  - *Transports* for delivery + ablative wounds — but **respect Ork anti-tank rokkits**;
    they're not invincible.
  - *Jump/fast* (Seraphim/Zephyrim) for the disruption/objective game + counter-charge.

**Detachment implication (firming up):** favour a rule that helps BOTH modes and doesn't
force close-range-only aggression — **Hallowed Martyrs** (below-strength +Hit, below-half
+Wound: flexible, rewards the casualties I'll take anyway) or **Champions of Faith**
(+1 BS to melta Battle Sisters/Paragons/Sacresants: sharper anti-tank). **Bringers of
Flame keeps sliding down** — it optimizes for the exact mono-flamer plan the meta punishes.
