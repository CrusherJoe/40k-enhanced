## Threat profile → what it forces MY Sisters list to do

**1. Durability I must punch through — SEVERE.**
2× Land Raider Redeemer (T12 W16 **2+**) + Gravis brick (T6 W6/W3) + 2+/4++ characters.
→ I need **melta (AP-4, Melta 2) in volume, on durable/mobile platforms**: multi-melta
Retributors, meltagun Dominion halves in Immolators, Paragon multi-meltas, inferno
pistols on characters. **Flamers (AP0/-1) bounce off 2+ saves** — they cannot crack this.
**This is "AP is KING" in the flesh.** To one-shot a Redeemer you need melta *within half
range* (Melta 2 → +2 dmg) and probably a Miracle die on the wound.

**2. What deletes my glass girls — a WALL of flamers.**
~20 pyreblasters + 4 flamestorm cannons + flamestorm gauntlets, ALL **Torrent (auto-hit)
+ Ignores Cover**. My T3 W1 Sv3+ bodies **auto-die with no cover save** the moment they
sit inside 12". → **Fight from transports (ablative wounds) and from melta range; kill
the Land Raiders before the flamer bricks disembark; never park bodies inside 12".**
A mirror flamer brawl **LOSES** — they out-flamer me and I'm the fragile side.
**⇒ Hard argument against an aggressive flamer Sisters list (Bringers of Flame / Purge).**

**3. Objective game — Reconnaissance.**
Fast cheap scorers (Outriders M12", jump Assault Intercessors M12") run
actions/Behind-Enemy-Lines while the durable center holds mid. → I need my own **fast
contesters** (Seraphim/Zephyrim jump, cheap OC Battle Sisters) to deny actions + **kill
their scoring pieces**, and win primary by out-positioning, not out-slugging.

## Net implication for the "best Sisters list"
Reinforces **melta-delivery + durability/transport-first**, and is a stark case *against*
the aggressive flamer build. My melta answers their Land Raiders/Gravis; my transports
keep fragile bodies alive through the flamer wall; my jump units contest their Recon
scoring. Detachment lean: a durability-realist rule (Hallowed Martyrs) over a
close-range-aggression rule (Bringers) — to be confirmed once more archetypes are in.
